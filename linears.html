Linear Switches

Among the enthusiasts, linear switches are considered to be the best. They offer a consistent, smooth travel have the best sound profile. Many users will painstakingly lube their switches to get the smoothest feel and they will tune their boards for hours to get the best sound.




Lubing switches helps you get the most smoothness and gives a deeper sound. The most common lubes for linears are Krytox 205g0 and Tribosys 3203.
